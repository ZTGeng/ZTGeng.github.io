Austerity Games
Here shows how games look like when visual designers are absent.


Go & Chess

Gomoku

The board is 15x15. Need two players.
No disallowed moves applied to Black.
Go

The board is 19x19. Need two players.
Chinese Chess

Need two players. Red first.
Chess

Need two players. White first.

Catch Cat

Catch Cat Original

Click on dots to block the cat.
You lose when cat reach the edge.
Catch Smelly Cat

Some of the dots contain cat poop. Don't click them.
Cat avoids poop dots.
Number on dot shows how many dots around it contain poop.
Right click to mark or unmark a dot.
Catch Two Cats

Two cats. Each moves one step every round.
You can click two dots in a round.
Cats never stay in the same dot.

Mini Games

Memory Game (Hard)

Match two grids with the same picture and color.
Matching will not happen before half of the grids are explored.
Rock Paper Scissors

Play rock-paper-scissors with computer.
Computer will show a big integer before you make a selection.
The reminder after division of the integer by 3 will be computer's selection.

Mine Sweeper

Mine Sweeper Original

Number show mines in the 8 grids around.
Right click to mark mine.
Mine Sweeper Liar

Some numbers may be wrong.
Right click to mark wrong numbers.
