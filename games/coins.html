Weight the Coins

  1. Select in your heart: Which coin has different weight with the others? Is it heavier or lighter?
    Do not change your selection during the game. Don't tell the computer your selection.
  2. Click "START". Check the coins on the pans and choose the correct move of the balance.
  3. After three rounds of weighing, the computer will guess your selection.
